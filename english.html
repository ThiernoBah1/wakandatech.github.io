News

Wakanda Technologies is authorized distributor of Arçelik products for West Africa. Arçelik is a global company offering electronic products that guarantee quality and reliability, and is one of the most popular brands in Eurasia.

In July this year, Wakanda Technologies will be opening her first electronics distribution store in an attractive location in Nigeria and later in Guinea.

The store design will be modern and attractive, resulting in a pleasant shopping for all our visitors. You can see the pictures of the store interior in the gallery.


In addition to a large number of the latest electronic models, a number of exciting new devices will also be available in the store: washing machine, pressing iron, cookers, refrigerators, coffee maker, microwave, intelligent home assistant, electric kettles.

One will also find smart devices such as dish washer, air conditioner, television, various high-tech lamps, and many other devices.

We believe that everyone will find something for themselves in our new store and we are looking forward to your visit!

Arçelik

Arçelik is a household electronic appliances manufacturer from Turkey. Arçelik was established in 1955 and its headquarters is in Istanbul, Turkey

The Company engages in the production and marketing of durable goods, components, consumer electronics and after-sale services


Arçelik today; worldwide 30,000 employees, Turkey, Romania, Russia, China, South Africa, Thailand and 18 manufacturing facilities in 7 countries, including Pakistan, with 33 sales and marketing offices and 11 brands in 31 countries (Arcelik, Beko, Grundig, Blomberg, Elektrabregenz Arctic, Leisure, Flavel, Defy, Altus and Dawlance) offer products and services in more than 130 countries.

Further growth is to be achieved by innovation, improved service quality and most importantly by producing new devices combining quality and affordability. The company's main goal is to provide users with innovations in line with leading trends on a monthly basis.
